Frequently Asked Questions

My notification verse and app verse don't match.
This was a known issue in v1 that has been fully resolved in v2. Please update to version 2.0 from the App Store. After updating, open the app once before your daily reminder time so the verse syncs correctly.
I am seeing the same verse two days in a row.
This has been fixed in v2. Please update to version 2.0 from the App Store. Force-quit the app and reopen it after updating to refresh the verse selection.
I am not receiving daily notifications.
Go to your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Pasian Kammal and make sure notifications are enabled. Then open the app, go to Settings, and confirm your reminder time is set and the toggle is on.
How do I copy a verse?
Press and hold the verse card for about half a second. The card will highlight and a "Copied" badge will appear — the verse text is now on your clipboard ready to paste anywhere.
How do I share a verse as an image?
Tap the Share button below the verse cards. The system share sheet will open with a beautifully designed verse image card ready to share to Messages, WhatsApp, or social media.
My favorites or reflections are missing after reinstalling.
Favorites and reflection notes are stored privately on your device and are not backed up to the cloud. They will not transfer if you reinstall the app or switch devices. We plan to add cloud backup in a future version.
I found a scripture error in the Zomi text.
Please email us at zomi7tech@gmail.com with the verse reference and the correction. We take scripture accuracy seriously and will review all submissions.
I have a feature suggestion.
We would love to hear it. Email us at zomi7tech@gmail.com with your idea. Pasian Kammal is built for the Zomi community and your feedback shapes every version.
