Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Zomi Laisiangtho does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything runs entirely on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how the Zomi Laisiangtho mobile application ("the App"), developed by Zomi7Tech ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or device location data
  • Your usage patterns, reading history, or search queries
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Any payment or financial information

2. How the App Works

Zomi Laisiangtho is a fully offline app. All content — including the Lai Siangtho Bible (66 books), Gal Hiam worship songs, and Tedim Labu hymns — is bundled directly into the app and stored on your device. No internet connection is required to read, search, or use any feature.

Your preferences (such as font size and appearance settings) are stored locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App currently does not integrate any third-party advertising networks or tracking services. If advertising is introduced in a future version, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes and describe what data may be collected by those services.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Those platforms may collect certain data as part of their standard operation (such as download counts and crash reports). Please refer to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies for more information:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at any time, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

5. Donations

The App includes an optional "Support Us" link that directs users to a third-party donation platform (Buy Me a Coffee). If you choose to donate, any information you provide is handled directly by that platform under their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store any of your payment information.
